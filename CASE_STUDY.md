# AgriSphere Case Study

## Problem
Farm operations were fragmented across paper logs and spreadsheets, causing:
- Data inconsistencies
- Missed vaccinations
- Poor yield forecasting
- Compliance risk

## Why This Was Hard
- Low tech literacy among staff
- Multiple crop and poultry lifecycles
- Strict EU traceability requirements
- Need for rapid delivery (3 weeks)

## My Role
Acted as Lead PM coordinating:
- Scope & backlog definition
- Stakeholder alignment
- Budibase delivery oversight
- Risk & change management

## Solution
Designed and delivered a unified farm management MVP using Budibase that:
- Centralized all data
- Automated reminders
- Provided real-time dashboards
- Supported regulatory compliance

## Impact
- Reduced manual logging errors
- Improved visibility into feed efficiency & yield
- Enabled faster operational decisions
